What we work for

Working for Social Cause and Welfare

JJYC envisions to empower youth, promote social justice, and inspire joy through collective action.

JJYC (Jan Jagaran Youth Club) stands as a dedicated force for social transformation and well-being. Through its unyielding commitment, JJYC tirelessly pursues the betterment of society by addressing pressing issues and uplifting underprivileged communities. The organization's initiatives encompass a wide range of areas, from advocating for justice and equality to fostering joy and empowerment among the youth. With a collaborative approach and a network of passionate volunteers, JJYC embodies the power of collective action in driving positive change. Its name itself reflects the dual pillars of its mission: to bring justice to the underserved and to infuse the journey with the joy that comes from creating a more just and equitable world.
